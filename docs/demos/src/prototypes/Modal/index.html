Small Modal

This is a smaller modal dialog, ideal for brief messages or simple confirmations that don't require much content space.

Large Modal

This is a larger modal dialog, suitable for detailed content, multi-column layouts, or data-heavy interfaces. The wider format gives breathing room for tables, images, or longer form content.

Large modals work well when users need to review substantial information before making a decision, such as comparing plans, reviewing detailed reports, or editing complex forms with many fields.

The maximum width ensures the modal remains readable and doesn't stretch uncomfortably wide on large displays, while the responsive max-width constraint keeps it usable on smaller viewports.
